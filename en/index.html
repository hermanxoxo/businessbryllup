Questions & answers

Frequently asked questions

What if we want to change something after launch?
Changes within the package are included through the agreed revision rounds. After launch, simple changes (text, images, schedule) are billed at 500 NOK/hour. Larger structural changes are quoted separately.
Do we own the site?
Yes — you get access to your own GitHub repo that belongs to you. You can take the site with you, give access to other developers, or simply leave it as it is.
How long will the site be available?
As long as you want. GitHub Pages is free and the site stays live until you actively delete the repo. There is no subscription fee from my side.
What about privacy and GDPR for RSVP data?
RSVP data (names, email, dietary needs) is stored in a Google Sheet that you own. No third party has access. We include a simple privacy policy on the site, and recommend that you delete the data after the wedding.
What if we don't have photos yet?
No problem. We use beautiful royalty-free placeholder images to start, and swap in your own photos when you have them — from engagement photos, the wedding photographer, or both.
Can we switch template midway through?
Until the content is filled in — yes, at no extra cost. After the site is finished, switching templates is treated as a new job and priced accordingly.
How long does it take?
From signed contract to live site typically takes 5–7 working days, provided you deliver the content promptly. The biggest variable is always waiting for text and photos from couples — the faster you deliver, the sooner the site is ready.
What if we want something that isn't in the packages?
Get in touch and tell me what you need. The Signature package is open to customisations, and simpler add-ons can be added to almost any package for an additional fee. I'm flexible — no two weddings are the same.
